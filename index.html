Anti-Cramp Protocol

What Happened Last Year

Your 2025 Spartan data tells a clear story: cardiovascular fitness was NOT the limiter. By miles 7-8, your HR dropped to the 130s while your pace cratered to 38-40 min/mile — your muscles were shutting down, not your heart. You spent 24% of Q2 above 170bpm with massive climbing, which cooked your quads before the second half.

Eccentric Training

  • Nordic Curls — 3x6-8, 2-3x/wk, progress to weighted
  • Slow Eccentric Squats — 3x6 with 5-sec lowering @65-70%
  • Downhill Running — 1x/wk, build from 200ft to 800ft+ descent
  • Eccentric Calf Raises — 3x15, 4-sec lowering

Electrolyte Protocol

  • Daily — 1,000-1,500mg sodium (LMNT or similar)
  • Night before race — 1,500-2,000mg sodium loading
  • Race morning — 500-800mg sodium + 16-20oz water
  • During race — Salt cap every 30-40 min, fuel every 45 min
  • Aid stations — Never skip, even in first half
